# The Problem

<v-clicks>

- **No pacing visibility** — how much time is left for this section?
- **No per-section tracking** — which parts ran over?
- **Post-talk mystery** — "What happened to my last 10 minutes?"
- **Rehearsal guesswork** — "How long will this talk actually take me?"
- Slidev's built-in timer shows total time, but not _where_ you are in the plan
